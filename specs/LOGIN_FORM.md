# Login Form

A centered login form with glassmorphic styling that floats above the animated background.

## Visual Design

### Form Container

- Centered horizontally and vertically in viewport
- Fixed width (~400px), auto height
- Semi-transparent purple/hot pink background
- Frosted glass blur effect (glassmorphism)
- Subtle white border, rounded corners
- Soft drop shadow

### Form Elements

1. **Title**: "Login" heading at top
2. **Email field**: Label "Email", hint text "you@example.com"
3. **Checkbox**: "Stay logged in" (default unchecked)
4. **Button**: "Continue" (full width, accent color)

## Behavior

- Form does NOT submit anywhere (display only)
- Remains centered at all viewport sizes
- Minimum viewport width: 320px
- Form appears above the scrolling background
