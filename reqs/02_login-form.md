# Login Form

Documents the visual appearance and structure of the glassmorphic login form including its container styling, form elements, and centering behavior.

## $REQ_LOGIN_FORM_001: Form Centered in Viewport
**Source:** ./specs/LOGIN_FORM.md (Section: "Form Container")

The login form container MUST be centered horizontally and vertically in the viewport.

## $REQ_LOGIN_FORM_002: Form Container Width
**Source:** ./specs/LOGIN_FORM.md (Section: "Form Container")

The login form container MUST have a fixed width of approximately 400 pixels with auto height.

## $REQ_LOGIN_FORM_003: Glassmorphic Background
**Source:** ./specs/LOGIN_FORM.md (Section: "Form Container")

The login form container MUST have a semi-transparent purple/hot pink background with a frosted glass blur effect (glassmorphism).

## $REQ_LOGIN_FORM_004: Form Container Border and Corners
**Source:** ./specs/LOGIN_FORM.md (Section: "Form Container")

The login form container MUST have a subtle white border and rounded corners.

## $REQ_LOGIN_FORM_005: Form Container Shadow
**Source:** ./specs/LOGIN_FORM.md (Section: "Form Container")

The login form container MUST have a soft drop shadow.

## $REQ_LOGIN_FORM_006: Login Title
**Source:** ./specs/LOGIN_FORM.md (Section: "Form Elements")

The login form MUST display a "Login" heading at the top.

## $REQ_LOGIN_FORM_007: Email Field
**Source:** ./specs/LOGIN_FORM.md (Section: "Form Elements")

The login form MUST include an email field with label "Email" and hint text "you@example.com".

## $REQ_LOGIN_FORM_008: Stay Logged In Checkbox
**Source:** ./specs/LOGIN_FORM.md (Section: "Form Elements")

The login form MUST include a "Stay logged in" checkbox that is unchecked by default.

## $REQ_LOGIN_FORM_009: Continue Button
**Source:** ./specs/LOGIN_FORM.md (Section: "Form Elements")

The login form MUST include a "Continue" button that is full width and uses an accent color.

## $REQ_LOGIN_FORM_010: Display Only
**Source:** ./specs/LOGIN_FORM.md (Section: "Behavior")

The login form MUST NOT submit anywhere (display only).

## $REQ_LOGIN_FORM_011: Centering at All Viewport Sizes
**Source:** ./specs/LOGIN_FORM.md (Section: "Behavior")

The login form MUST remain centered at all viewport sizes with a minimum supported viewport width of 320 pixels.

## $REQ_LOGIN_FORM_012: Form Above Background
**Source:** ./specs/LOGIN_FORM.md (Section: "Behavior")

The login form MUST appear above the scrolling background.
